Chapter 09
Unlocking joy

Receptional Design

Design moments of joy at the point of reception.

Receptional design transforms your customer's emotional state at the precise moment of engagement. It unlocks a moment of joy. It calms a moment of anxiety. It energizes a moment of learning. Emotions live in the brain — serotonin rises as anxiety drops, dopamine fires on achievement, endorphins energize, oxytocin builds trust.

a tiny moment of joy
Not designing for conception.
Designing for reception.
Omotenashi →
The Japanese concept of Omotenashi — to wholeheartedly look after your guest. Reception and exceptional, fused. The invisible micro-moments that make a guest feel anticipated, cared for, known.

Are you designing the invisible micro-moments, not just the experience itself?

Chapter 10
Are you paying attention?

Engineering
for Virality

Build what spreads & amplify what's already moving.

Virality is behavior designed into the experience that people choose to share and propagate. It is not a great ad. It is not a big influencer. It is not paid distribution. It is the architecture of identity, emotion, and participation — engineered so sharing feels like self-expression.

Virality = Identity × Emotion × Participation
The pattern underneath →
Participation ritual — belonging and FOMO simultaneously.

Personalization — identity expression disguised as sharing.

Conflict / sides — tribal loyalty and outrage both spread.

Brand character — entertainment value that earns attention.

Real-time relevance — first-mover cultural credibility.

Creative tool — the audience becomes the distribution engine.

What part of this makes someone look good sharing it? Where is the emotional spike?
